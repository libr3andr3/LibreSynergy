# Install the app

Add this app to your home screen — it opens full-screen, loads instantly, and works even with a spotty connection. No app store needed.

## iPhone / iPad (Safari)
1. Open this site in **Safari**.
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with an arrow, bottom of the screen).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**, then **Add**.

## Android (Chrome)
1. Open this site in **Chrome**.
2. Tap the **⋮ menu** (top right) → **Install app** (or **Add to Home screen**).
3. Confirm with **Install**.

## Desktop (Chrome / Edge / Brave)
1. Look for the **install icon** in the address bar (a monitor with a down arrow).
2. Click it, then **Install**. The app gets its own window and dock/taskbar icon.

> Firefox and Safari on desktop don't support installing — just bookmark the page.
